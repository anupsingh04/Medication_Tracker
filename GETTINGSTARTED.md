## Introduction
Medimate is your personal health assistant. It helps you keep track of your medication schedule, find information about different pills, and monitor your adherence to your prescription plan.

## Getting Started
For ease of use, Medimate does not require a login. When you open the app, you will be taken directly to the Home Dashboard, ready to start managing your medications immediately.

## Home Dashboard
The Home screen is your daily command center.
- **Greeting:** Personalized welcome message.
- **Upcoming To-do List:** Shows your next scheduled medication. Tap on it to mark it as taken (feature coming soon) or view details.
- **Medical Journal:** A quick link to log any side effects you might be feeling. It's important to keep track of how you feel!
- **News/Articles:** Stay updated with the latest health news. You can switch between "Latest" news and your "Bookmarked" articles.

## Searching for Medication
Navigate to the **Search** tab (magnifying glass icon) to find medicines.
- **By Name:** Type the name of the medication in the search bar.
- **By Appearance:** If you have a loose pill and don't know what it is, use the "Search by Pill Characteristic" section. Select the **Color** (e.g., White, Red) and **Shape** (e.g., Round, Oval) to find matches.
- **My Meds:** Below the search, you can see tabs for "Currently Taking" (active prescriptions) and "Non-active" (past medications).
- **Help:** Tap the question mark icon for a guide on "How to swallow pills" if you have difficulty.

## Managing Your Medications
Go to the **Medication** tab (pill icon) to view your full list.
- **Active Meds:** Displays all medications you are currently taking.
- **View Details:** Tap on a medication card to see dosage, provider info, and your adherence stats.
- **Add Medication:** Tap the **+ (Plus)** button to add a new prescription. You'll be guided through a few steps to enter the name, dosage, and schedule.
- **History:** Tap the **History** button to view your past logs and journals.
- **Delete:** Tap the **Delete** button on a medication card to remove it.

## Viewing Your Schedule
The **Schedule** tab (calendar icon) helps you plan your day.
- **Calendar:** Select a date to view the schedule for that specific day.
- **Daily Timeline:** The list below the calendar shows your meds grouped by meal times (e.g., Before Breakfast, After Lunch, Bedtime).
    - **Yellow:** Morning/Breakfast times.
    - **Light Blue:** Afternoon/Lunch times.
    - **Dark Blue:** Evening/Dinner & Bedtime.

## Profile & Settings
The **Profile** tab (person icon) holds your personal information.
- **Profile:** View your personal details like Name, Age, Height, Weight, and Blood Type.
- **Settings:** Access app preferences (Notifications, Account Privacy, Help & Support).
- **Edit Mode:** Tap the **Edit** (pencil) icon in the top right corner to make changes to your profile.
